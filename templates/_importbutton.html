Welcome to the page for drawing graphs with Baguette

For drawing, you have two options:

  • You can use the buttons on the left-hand side to draw the graph.
  • Alternatively, you can use the contextual menu, which provides options for creating edges and deleting edges, as well as deleting or updating selected nodes. Only the create button and the delete edge who must be used without the contextual menu

To create a node, click on the "Create Node" button. A dialog will appear, asking you to enter the node's name and type. After entering the details, click on the "Create" button. The cursor will change to a crosshair, and the page will wait for you to click on a location in the table. Once you click on a location, the node will be created at that precise spot, and the cursor will return to normal mode. If you wish to cancel the creation, simply click outside the drawing board, and the cursor will revert to automatic mode, canceling the operation.

For other operations, such as creating edges ..., once you click for a button the cursor will, sameway, change to a crosshair the page will wait until you click on the node (or edge) to handle. Follow the provided steps to perform the desired operation. If you decide not to proceed with the operation after clicking the corresponding button, simply click on an empty area or outside the drawing board, and the cursor will return to its previous state.

I hope you have a great experience using this graph drawing tool.
